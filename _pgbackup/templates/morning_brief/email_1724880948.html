What Did the Survey Reveal?

  • Opening Ceremony Controversy: A significant 54% of respondents found the opening ceremony highly inappropriate, raising questions about the role of controversial elements in global events.

  • Canadian Team Performance: While 70% of Canadians were satisfied with their team's performance, about 60% expressed concern about the drone spying scandal affecting the perception of Canadian sports.

  • Media Coverage: More than a third of respondents rated the media coverage of the Paris Olympics as "poor" or "very poor," raising concerns about bias and ethics. Do you share these concerns and see the need for an independent and ethical news source?
